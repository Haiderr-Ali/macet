About The College:

The “Maulana Azad College of Engineering & Technology″ was started in the year 1987 under the Millat Education Society. This was the first technical self-financed minority institution in the entire northern India. MACET was set up keeping in view the rapid technological growth and changes taking place in the industry. The students at MACET have the best of facilities, a conducive environment for studies and a dedicated faculty to guide them and lead them to success.

Courses Offered:

  • B.tech/B.E in-
    • Electrical and electronics engineering
    • Computer science and engineering
    • Electronics and communication engineering
    • Mechanical engineering
    • Civil engineering
    • Artificial intelligence/Machine learning
    • Data science
